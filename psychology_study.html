Study Instructions

Thank you for participating in this psychology study. Please follow these steps:

  1. First, click the TEST (run first) button to initialize the study.
  2. After completing the test, click the Begin Study button to start the full study.
  3. Follow the on-screen instructions for each part of the study.
  4. At the end, you'll have the option to download your data.

Important: You must click the TEST button first before beginning the study.
